## ✨ Such A Little Gem

> _An ephemeral blaze in your browser._

An interactive, gem-themed experience built for the web.  
All visuals are crafted using pure HTML and CSS, exploring light, material, and motion in a digital space.

---

## 🌐 Overview

This is a visual and interaction-driven experimental website rather than a traditional functional project.

Inside, you can:

- Scroll horizontally through a curated series of gems
- Observe each gem rendered entirely with **HTML / CSS (no images)**
- Experience subtle shimmer, glow, and refraction effects
- Interact through click, double-click, and drag gestures

The project aims to evoke the feeling of:

> **Handling and observing gems within a digital environment**

---

## 🎮 Interaction

### 🧭 Horizontal Navigation

The layout is designed as a horizontal scroll experience, allowing you to move through each gem sequentially.

---

### 💎 Double Click — Palette View

Double-click on a gem to:

- Expand into its **palette view**
- Reveal its color composition and structural highlights
- Shift from object-level viewing to a more design-oriented perspective

---

### 🖐️ Drag — Spatial Interaction

Dragging a gem will:

- Reveal a **background array of gems**
- Trigger a **parallax effect**
- Introduce depth and spatial layering to the scene

---

### ✨ Hover & Motion

- Gems respond to hover interactions
- Highlights, reflections, and glow dynamically shift
- Motion is designed to feel light, brief, and luminous

## 🧪 Tech & Approach

### Rendering

- Pure **HTML / CSS**
- Built using gradients, clip-path, transforms, and layering
- No bitmap assets for gem rendering

---

### Animation

- CSS animations and JavaScript-driven motion
- Emphasis on shimmer, glow, and light transitions

---

### Interaction Model

- Pointer-based interactions (hover, drag, double-click)
- Multi-state transitions between browsing and focused views
